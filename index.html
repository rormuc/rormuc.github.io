Refugees on Rails is a Coding School for refugees organized by a group of volunteers.

// About

We are the Munich chapter of Refugees on Rails. With the help of many volunteers we have set up a 15 week Ruby on Rails course for refugees in the Munich vicinity.

// Student

Are you interested in learning Ruby on Rails? Then sign up now and we will get in touch with you. Don’t worry, you don’t need any previous programming skills in order to complete this course.

You do however need to spend roughly 8 hours a week on studying on top of the 4 hours you’ll spend in class each week for 15 weeks.
